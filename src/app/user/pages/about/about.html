Our Story

From a Small Nursery
to a Trusted Brand

Founded in 2009, Urban Plants started with a simple mission — make quality plants and seeds accessible to everyone.

What began as a small family-owned nursery has grown into a trusted source for gardeners across the country. Our passion for plants and commitment to sustainability drives everything we do.

We believe everyone deserves access to fresh, healthy plants and high-quality seeds — whether you're a seasoned gardener or just starting out.
